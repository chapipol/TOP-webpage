It's audio getting better?

Throughout the history of audio storage, different devices have been developed. Some became obsolete and others are still used today.

Audio storage

Phonograph cylinders are the earliest commercial medium for recording and reproducing sound

Vinyl is an analog sound storage medium in the form of a flat disc with an inscribed, modulated spiral groove

The cassette tape, audio cassette, or simply tape or cassette, is an analog magnetic tape recording format for audio recording and playback.

The compact disc (CD) is a digital optical disc data storage format that was co-developed by Philips and Sony to store and play digital audio recordings
